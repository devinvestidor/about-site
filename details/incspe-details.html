INCPSE

My project for my degree in Information Technology Management. The game consists of a challenge of 10 questions, where the user must answer according to the sum of two numbers that are presented. In addition, a primary color composition scheme was developed, where the system randomly draws two colors for the question and provides the color with the respective answer in the result. At the end of the game, information such as: number of hits, number of errors, total activity time, name of the game and player name are inserted into the database, for possible consultations in the future.
